THE RESIDENCES DISTRICT

Al Widyan has housing to suit every style, ranging from luxury villas
and premium townhouses to contemporary flats. Each home is fitted
with modern amenities, including mobile controlled room
temperatures and car recognition. All are beautifully complemented
by natural wadi streams, winding paths and extensive
neighbourhood parks,
creating a true sense of Saudi community.

RIYADH HILLS

Riyadh Hills is a new model for luxury, low-rise residences, seamlessly integrating sophisticated villas, premium townhouses and modern flats, all with easy access to Central Park, Town Centre and a variety of retail and entertainment options. The residences offer private parking and freehold ownership.

The Residences

PALACES

Destined to be one of the Kingdoms most sought-after addresses, the Palaces are exclusively unique, inspired by legendary heritage, influenced by iconic fashion trends and built by legendary craftsmanship. These homes perfectly balance elegance with artistry, extravagance and ambiance, resulting in exquisite homes that are to be savoured.

LUXURY VILLAS

Ofering uncompromising elegance, these handcrafted homes are simultaneously contemporary and comfortable. They offer a full concierge service, valet parking and 24-hour security for extra peace of mind.

PREMIUM VILLAS

Establishing a new standard for urban living, these villas perfectly harmonise modernity and comfort, creating a living experience that appeals to all lifestyles.

TOWNHOUSES

Providing the perfect cosmopolitan address, these Townhouses are elegantly equipped and furnished, and provide a world-class concierge service that matches this abode to the finest luxury lifestyle.
