THE HEALTHCARE DISTRICT

Promotion of personal health is at the forefront of Al Widyan’s desire
to foster wellbeing throughout the community, with top-of-the-line
hospitals and clinics accommodating every health care need and
eventuality, completely furnished with state-of-the-art equipment.
Fitness and relaxation are also accommodated for, with multiple
sports facilities coupled with world-class spas
and wellness centres.

RMULTIDISCIPLINARY MEDICAL CENTRE

World-class internationally led institution outlining the new health-care standard in the Kingdom, with highly specialised, state-of-the-art services.

  • Specialist paediatric centre
  • Women’s healthcare centre
  • 24-hour surgical care centre
  • Chronic disease management centre
  • Multi-disciplined medical complex

LONG-TERM CARE COMMUNITY

Long-term care and hospice services to help support and care for any residents who are in need of full-time assistance.

  • Baseline assisted living
  • Heightened medical and wellness support
  • Intensive care for patients in need of special medical attention
  • First of its kind Saudi Arabian hospice centre

REHABILITATION CENTRE

Providing the highest standard of experience and care to patients in need of rehabilitation.

  • Physical rehabilitation services
  • Doctors, nurses and staff accommodation
  • Latest recuperative methodology and techniques

RESEARCH AND DEVELOPMENT

  • Stem cell research
  • Modern medical technology
  • Bio engineering
  • Genomics
  • Pharmaceuticals

MEDICAL DIAGNOSTIC CLINIC

  • Medical services based on regional demand
  • Plastic surgery, dermatology and aesthetics
  • Point-of-care diagnostics
  • Home based analysis, diagnostics and remote treatment
  • Diagnostic testing service
  • Corrective eye surgery
  • Compounding pharmacy
